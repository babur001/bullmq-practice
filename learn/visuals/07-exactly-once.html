Worker A claims a job under a lock with a TTL (QULF), charges a card (TO'LOV), then must ack "done" to Redis. If A stalls in the gap between charging and acking, the lock expires, Redis hands the same job to Worker B, and B charges again — unless a consumer-side idempotency guard (KAFOLAT, atomic SET NX) stops it.
